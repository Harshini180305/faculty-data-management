National Board of Accreditation (NBA)

NBA accredits technical programs that meet quality standards and promote excellence in engineering and technical education. It ensures institutions meet industry-relevant competencies and continuous improvement.

National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC)

NAAC evaluates higher education institutions for their quality in terms of curriculum, faculty, infrastructure, research, and innovation, aiming to uplift the overall standards of education in India.

All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

AICTE is the national-level apex advisory body for technical education in India. It ensures proper planning and coordinated development of technical education across the country through regulation and accreditation.
